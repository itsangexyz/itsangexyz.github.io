Summary of the Story

Solo Leveling follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter in a world where magical gates connect Earth to deadly dungeons filled with monsters. After surviving a disastrous mission, he gains a mysterious ability to grow stronger by leveling up—something no one else can do. As his strength increases, he commands a powerful shadow army and uncovers the secrets behind the gates, his powers, and a looming war between godlike beings. His journey transforms him from a powerless fighter into humanity’s strongest protector.
